mailto: Helper Service

Trying to use mailto: links, but not ending up in the email application where you need to be? mailthat.org is the solution!

To use this free service, add https://mailthat.org/? in front of your mailto: links. Users will be given multiple options to send their email exactly how they want, including many common email apps and services.

Demo

Given mailto:demo@mailthat.org, your mailthat.org link would be
https://mailthat.org?mailto:demo@mailthat.org

Security and Privacy

This service works client-side in your web browser; information is not processed or stored by the web server except as necessary to deliver the web page to you and otherwise operate the service. There are no cookies and no third-party trackers. Your information is not sold.
